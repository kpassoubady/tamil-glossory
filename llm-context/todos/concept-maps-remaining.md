# Concept Map Images — Remaining Chapters (06-11)

## Master Prompt

Use this as the base for every chapter concept map image. Append the chapter-specific section below it.

> **CRITICAL:** Do NOT include any Tamil script, Tamil letters (e.g. "அ", "தமிழ்"), or any text/words of any language rendered inside the image. LLMs generate Tamil text poorly and it will look broken. The image must be purely visual — no text overlays, no labels, no captions, no watermarks.

```text
Create a professional, highly conceptual editorial illustration for a Tamil AI glossary book.
This image serves as an abstract visual synthesis (infographic-inspired art) placed at the end of a chapter, metaphorically illustrating how the key ideas interconnect. Do NOT draw a literal diagram, text boxes, or whiteboard map.

Style requirements:
- Editorial vector illustration style, minimalist, elegant, and highly structured, utilizing soft gradients, clean geometric lines, and balanced negative space.
- Color palette: Sleek, analytical cool tones (deep indigo/navy blue #1b263b, slate gray) contrasted with luminous cyan, soft teal, and warm gold as the primary focal accents. This palette should look distinctly different from the green/earth-tone hero images, giving these concept maps a more 'structured data' and 'analytical' vibe.
- 6×9 aspect ratio, landscape orientation.
- Strictly ZERO TEXT. No text, no labels, no letters, no words, no numbers, and no captions of any language anywhere in the image.
- Use beautiful abstract shapes, distinct color groupings, and elegant spatial arrangement to convey relationships (hierarchy, flow, contrast, clustering) visually without needing words.
- Cultural integration: Seamlessly weave traditional Tamil cultural motifs (such as kolam/pulli dot-grids, temple gopuram structural geometry, or the elegant curves of traditional brass artifacts) into the structural foundation of the image. Use these motifs to represent networks and connections.
- Maintain a consistent, premium, and sophisticated art style across all chapters.
```
