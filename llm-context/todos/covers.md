## Front Cover Prompt

> **Design a premium Tamil AI book cover** with a deep green (#1a4d2e) to black gradient background. The cover should feel modern, tech-forward, and distinctly Tamil.
>
> **Title (largest text, centered, top 40%):**
> `AI அலை: நீங்கள் தயாரா?`
> — Bold, white, modern Tamil font, prominent and commanding. This is the hero text.
>
> **Subtitle (smaller, below title, centered):**
> `தமிழில் 300+ AI கலைச்சொற்கள்`
> — Light green (#a5d6a7) or muted gold, approximately 40% the size of the title.
>
> **Visual elements (middle zone, 30-50% of cover):**
> A stylized wave (அலை) made of glowing circuit traces and neural network nodes, flowing left to right. Inside the wave, subtle Tamil script characters (அ, ஆ, இ) float as data particles. The wave transitions from traditional Kolam-pattern geometry on the left into futuristic AI neural patterns on the right — symbolizing Tamil heritage meeting AI future. Tiny icons of a brain, chip, and chat bubble embedded within the wave nodes. Deep green (#1a4d2e) glow with accents of electric teal and muted gold.
>
> **Author name (bottom, centered):**
> `காங்கேயன் பசுபதி`
> — White or light gold, clean modern font, smaller than subtitle, with a thin horizontal line separator above it.
>
> **Style:** Flat vector with subtle depth, no photorealism. Clean, minimal, high contrast. The cover should make someone stop scrolling and want to open the book. No English text anywhere on the cover. Aspect ratio 6:9 (standard book cover). Print-ready, 300 DPI.
